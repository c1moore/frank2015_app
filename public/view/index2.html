frank 2015 app

On February 24, the frank 2015 app will go live, but why wait until then? Bookmark this page for easy access during the event. To save the webpage on your homescreen, follow these instructions:

iPhone/iPad

These instructions only work with the Safari app. You can bookmark the app in any browser, but you cannot add it to your homescreen.

  1. Launch Safari and navigate to this address (www.frank2015.net).
  2. Press the share button (the rectangle with an arrow pointing up out of it).
  3. Select Add to Home Screen from the menu that appears.
  4. Enter a name for the app (we suggest frank 2015).
  5. Tap Add
  6. You can now access the frank app from your home screen.

Android

For the best user experience, we suggest viewing the site on Chrome.


Chrome for Android
  1. Launch Chrome for Android and navigate to this address (www.frank2015.net).
  2. Tap the menu button (the three square boxes lined up vertically).
  3. Select Add to homescreen.
  4. Enter a name for the app (we suggest frank 2015).
  5. You can now access the frank app from your homescreen.
Firefox
  1. Launch Firefox and navigate to this address (www.frank2015.net).
  2. Tap the menu button.
  3. Select the star at the top of the menu.
  4. After the bookmark is added, tap Options on the popup that appears near the bottom of the screen.
  5. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  6. You can now access the frank app from your homescreen.
